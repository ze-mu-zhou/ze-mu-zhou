## 01 / Hi, I'm zemu

A down-to-earth university student who keeps wandering through the digital world.
Fan of CTF challenges, reverse engineering, binary exploitation, and a little bit of controlled chaos.
Welcome to my GitHub, where notes, scripts, and weird bugs slowly become reusable tools.
If something here helps you, a star is always appreciated.

### About Me

- I'm currently studying Software Engineering at Northeastern University, China, with a security and reversing focus.
- I enjoy CTF challenges, pwn, IDA Pro automation, and learning sharp new tricks.

- Fueled by curiosity, late-night debugging, and occasionally too much coffee.
